## The PsycheMap Project

**PsycheMap** is a research-driven initiative focused on the intersection of **quantitative psychology** and **data science**. The project aims to decode complex emotional patterns through longitudinal data tracking and algorithmic analysis.

By leveraging computational tools, we transform subjective experiences into structured data to identify cycles, triggers, and stability trends in human behavior.

You can access the live application here: **[PsycheMap App](https://www.psychemapapp.com/app)**

## Methodology and Technology

The core of this project relies on a technical stack designed for precision and privacy:

* **Predictive Modeling:** Utilizing machine learning algorithms, such as **Random Forest Regressors**, to project emotional trends.
* **Signal Processing:** Implementing **Exponential Moving Averages (EMA)** and peak detection to filter daily noise and identify significant psychological "peaks and valleys."
* **Data Integrity:** A commitment to **Privacy-First** architecture, ensuring that all analyzed metrics remain encrypted and under the user's control.
* **Environmental Stack:** Developed within a **Linux (Pop!_OS)** environment using **Python** for backend analysis and **Hugo** for data visualization and documentation.
